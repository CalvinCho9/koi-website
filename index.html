Welcome to KOI

An inclusive world starts with understanding.

KOI works to empower individuals with special needs and promote neurodiversity within education systems. As a nonprofit 501(c)(3) organization based in Orange County, we create inclusive learning environments and raise awareness through educational workshops, the development of neurodiverse resources, and volunteer support in special-needs classrooms.

Our goal is to foster understanding, acceptance, and opportunity for all.

What we do

Two ways we make neurodiversity easier to understand.

Children's education

Neurodiverse Glossary

Our children's glossary covers over 30 terms related to neurodiversity — including ADHD, autism, and dyslexia — written in simple language with relatable examples so young readers can grasp complex ideas.

Therapy series

Math + Art (M-ART) Workbooks

Our curated M-ART workbooks blend everyday math — counting, money, cooking — with creative expression. They're designed to build cognitive skills, encourage self-expression, and make learning genuinely enjoyable.
